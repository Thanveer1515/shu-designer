Our Craft
The Art of Making
01
🐄
Leather Sourcing
Full-grain and top-grain leather from certified tanneries in Italy and Tamil Nadu. Vegetable-tanned for longevity and character.
02
✂️
Hand Cutting
Each pattern is hand-cut by master craftsmen using steel dies. No two cuts are identical — every pair is truly one of a kind.
03
🪡
Hand Stitching
Traditional welt construction. Each shoe receives over 200 individual hand stitches in our Chennai workshop over 3 full days.
04
✨
Finishing
Three coats of hand-applied polish, edge finishing, and final quality inspection before your pair is boxed and dispatched.

Return Policy
🛒 Return & Exchange Policy

Here’s a clear and professional Return Policy for our handcrafted leather footwear — designed to balance customer trust with business protection.

Eligibility for Returns
  • Products can be returned within 14 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unused, unworn, and in original packaging with tags intact.
  • Proof of purchase (order confirmation or invoice) is required.
Non-Returnable Items
  • Shoes that show signs of wear, damage, or alteration.
  • Customized or discounted items (unless defective).
Exchanges
  • Exchanges are allowed for size or color variations, subject to stock availability.
  • If the requested size/color is unavailable, a refund will be issued.
Refunds
  • Refunds will be processed within 7–10 business days after inspection.
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method only.
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the item was defective or incorrect.
Defective or Wrong Item
  • If you receive a defective or incorrect product, contact us within 48 hours of delivery.
  • We will arrange a free replacement or full refund.
Return Process
  1. Contact our support team via email or phone with your order details.
  2. Pack the shoes securely in the original box.
  3. Ship the item to the provided return address.
  4. Once received and inspected, we’ll notify you about your refund/exchange status.
Refund Policy
💳 Refund Policy

Here’s a professional Refund Policy for our handcrafted leather footwear — customer-friendly while protecting the business.

Refund Eligibility
  • Refunds are available within 14 days of delivery.
  • Shoes must be unused, unworn, and in original packaging with all tags intact.
  • Proof of purchase (order confirmation or invoice) is required.
Refund Process
  1. Contact our support team with your order details.
  2. Return the product securely in its original box.
  3. Once inspected and approved, we will initiate the refund.
Refund Timeline
  • Refunds are processed within 7–10 business days after we receive and inspect the item.
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method only.
Shipping & Handling
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable, unless the item was defective or incorrect.
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer, except in cases of defective or wrong items.
Defective or Wrong Item
  • If you receive a defective or incorrect product, notify us within 48 hours of delivery.
  • We will arrange a free replacement or issue a full refund.
Shipping Information
🚚 Shipping Information

Clear shipping expectations for our handcrafted leather footwear.

Processing Time
  • Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation.
  • Customized or special orders may take longer; customers will be notified.
Shipping Methods
  • We partner with trusted courier services to ensure safe and timely delivery.
  • Standard shipping: 5–7 business days.
  • Express shipping: 2–4 business days (additional charges apply).
Shipping Charges
  • Standard shipping is free for orders above ₹2,000 (or equivalent).
  • For orders below this amount, a flat shipping fee will be applied at checkout.
International Shipping
  • Available to select countries.
  • Delivery times vary by destination (usually 7–14 business days).
  • Customs duties or import taxes are the responsibility of the customer.
Order Tracking
  • Once shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email/SMS.
  • You can track your order directly on our courier partner’s website.
Delays
  • While we strive for timely delivery, unforeseen circumstances (weather, strikes, customs delays) may affect shipping times.
  • We will keep you updated in case of any delays.
